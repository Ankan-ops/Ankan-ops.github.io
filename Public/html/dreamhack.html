This is DreamHack


Dreamhack is the worlds largest Local Area Network event (or LAN for short).
The event was founded 1992 and became a big hit in sweden between 1997 and 2001. The event focuses on the aspect of gaming and the whole community around it. Recently the gaming company Named: ESL Gaming merged together with DreamHack and function as one. On the other hand they have their seperate divisions and operate on different events.
